Saigon Hi-Tech Park Incubation Center (SHTP-IC)

http://www.eng.shtp.hochiminhcity.gov.vn/

Saigon Hi-Tech Park Incubation Center (“SHTP-IC”), a subsidiary of the ManagementBoard of Saigon Hi-Tech Park (“SHTP”), is not-for-profit organization, was established on August 28th ,2006. SHTP-IC not only determinates the successful development of startups and fledging companies, but is also the guideline for all activities referred to as “clients”.

SHTP-IC has been promoted the concept of growth innovation and application of technology, scientific ideas or prototypes to become perfect technologies, supports economic development strategies for small business development.
